Blending Brilliance

Date: 04-03-2024

Venue: E-learning Center

The event titled "Blending Brilliance" featured a comprehensive workshop on the versatile software, Blender, conducted on March 4th, 2024, by esteemed resource persons Ahmed Thameem, Mohammed Thameem Raz, Abdul Mousooq, and Abdul Nafih from 3rd years CSE, alongside the leading members of GLUG Pace, Muhammed Ajlan, Muadh Bin Mohammed Ali, and Shakeel Ahammed Adullah. The day commenced with the installation of Blender on participants' PCs, setting the stage for an immersive learning experience. From 9:30 to 10:00 am, Mohammed Thameem Raz provided a captivating introduction to Blender, delving into its rich history and significance in the digital realm.

Following this, Ahmed Thameem took the reins, guiding attendees through the fundamentals of Blender and elucidating on the intricacies of model creation from 10:00 am to 11:10 am. The session after the break, extending from 11:30 am to 1:10 pm, saw Thameem's expert demonstration on building a virtual coffee shop, capturing the attention of all with practical insights and techniques.

As the day progressed, participants delved deeper into Blender's capabilities, with a focus on coloring and shading the coffee shop model during the afternoon session. Moreover, in an engaging twist, students were challenged with random questions about Blender during this session and were rewarded with chocolates for their participation, enhancing the interactive nature of the workshop. Ahmed Thameem concluded the workshop on a high note by showcasing captivating animations and rendering techniques, leaving attendees inspired and eager to explore further.

The event's success was further underscored by a constructive feedback session led by Hafeez Sir at 3:50 pm, providing participants with an opportunity to share their insights and suggestions. Certificates of appreciation were presented to the resource persons, acknowledging their invaluable contributions. The event culminated in a memorable group photo, capturing the camaraderie and shared enthusiasm for Blender among all participants.

Additionally, participants received e-certificates via email, serving as a testament to their active participation and commitment to enhancing their skills in Blender. Overall, "Blending Brilliance" proved to be a transformative learning experience, empowering participants with practical knowledge and inspiration to leverage Blender's capabilities creatively.
