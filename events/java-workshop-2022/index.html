Java Workshop

Date: 5-12-2022

Venue: Multipurpose Hall

GLUG Pace, Department of Computer Science and Engineering is organizing a 2 days workshop(for 3rd sem) on Java on 5th December 2022 and 7th December 2022 from 9am-4pm, in the Multipurpose Hall.

The workshop will be covering basic concepts of java. Like how variables, condition, loops and functions work.

This is be a complete hands on workshop where the students can bring their laptops and work along with the instructors.
